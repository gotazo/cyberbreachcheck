## What is a phishing email?

A phishing email is designed to trick you into:
- clicking malicious links  
- entering login credentials  
- downloading malware  

---

## Common signs of phishing emails

### 1. Urgent language
- "Your account is locked"
- "Immediate action required"

---

### 2. Suspicious sender address
Check the email carefully:
- support@paypa1.com ❌
- support@paypal.com ✅

---

### 3. Generic greetings
- "Dear user"
- "Dear customer"

---

### 4. Suspicious links
Always verify links before clicking.
