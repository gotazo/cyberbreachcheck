## What is a fake website?

A fake website is designed to look like a real one to trick you into:
- entering your login details  
- making payments  
- downloading malware  

---

## How to check if a website is legit

### 1. Check the domain name
Look for small changes:
- paypaI.com ❌  
- paypal.com ✅
