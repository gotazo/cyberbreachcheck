## What is a phishing link?

A phishing link is a malicious URL designed to:
- steal your login credentials  
- install malware  
- redirect you to fake websites  

---

## What happens when you click a phishing link?

### 1. You may be redirected to a fake website
These sites look real but are designed to steal your data.

---

### 2. Your credentials may be stolen
If you enter your login details:
- hackers can access your accounts  
- your email and passwords may be compromised  

---

### 3. Malware may be installed
Some links automatically download harmful software.

---

### 4. Your device may be tracked
Hackers can collect:
- your IP address  
- your location  
- device information
